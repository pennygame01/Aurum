Google sign-in: auth:store — “ID wasn't valid base32” on db.get(verifier)
==========================================================================

Convex Auth stores OAuth state under authVerifiers. The Next handler passes `verifier` as the Convex document id.

Typical causes:
- NEXT_PUBLIC_CONVEX_URL (or deployed frontend env) points at a different Convex deployment than the one issuing cookies — verifier ids belong to another DB.
- Stale cookies after switching Convex deployment or SITE_URL — clear site cookies for your Penny domain and try again.
- CONVEX_SITE_URL / Auth proxy misconfigured vs production URLs.

Fix: align frontend Convex URL with the Convex project hosting authTables; redeploy; users clear cookies once.
